## Customization Variables

Replace these based on target project:

- `{PROJECT_NAME}` - e.g., "Rainmaker", "Pyro"
- `{TOKEN_SYMBOL}` - e.g., "$RAIN", "$PYRO"
- `{MIGRATION_DATE}` - e.g., "Dec 9th", "Dec 16th"
- `{PARTICIPATION_RATE}` - e.g., "20%", "56%"
- `{EXISTING_CLIENT}` - "PAYAI", "ZERA", or "other projects"

## Best Practices

- Send between 9AM-5PM EST (crypto hours)
- Wait 3-5 days before follow-up
- Reference their specific situation
- Keep it under 280 characters when possible
- Don't pitch pricing in first message
